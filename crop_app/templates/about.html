What We Do

Our platform uses deep learning models trained on the New Plant Disease Dataset from Kaggle to analyze plant leaf images and detect diseases with high accuracy. Once detected, it provides easy-to-follow prevention steps to control and manage the disease.

How It Works

  • You upload a clear image of the affected plant leaf.
  • Our AI model processes the image and predicts the disease based on visual patterns.
  • We show you the name of the disease, detailed description, and step-by-step prevention tips.
  • We also display reference images to help you visually verify the diagnosis.

Why This Matters

Agriculture feeds the world. Our goal is to empower farmers with modern technology to protect their crops, improve yields, and reduce chemical usage by enabling early intervention.

This system helps:

  • Farmers to make timely decisions and avoid crop loss
  • Gardeners to keep their plants healthy
  • Students and researchers to explore practical AI applications in agriculture
